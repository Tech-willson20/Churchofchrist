About Our Church

Walking in Faith, Growing in Grace

Our Story

The Church of Christ has been serving our community with unwavering dedication to spreading God's Word since its establishment. Our congregation began with a small group of devoted believers committed to restoring New Testament Christianity, and has grown into a vibrant community of faith.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples who love God, love people, and serve both with the message and love of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church that impacts our community and beyond with the gospel, while nurturing spiritual growth and fostering authentic relationships.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Biblical, Christ-centered teaching
  • Warm, welcoming community
  • Multi-generational ministry
  • Strong emphasis on discipleship
  • Active community engagement
  • Focus on family values
  • Contemporary & traditional worship

Our Core Values

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our ultimate authority for faith and practice. All our teachings and decisions are grounded in Scripture.

Authentic Community

We foster genuine relationships where people can find acceptance, support, and accountability as they grow in their faith journey.

Intentional Discipleship

We are committed to helping each person grow in their relationship with Christ through teaching, mentoring, and practical application of God's Word.
