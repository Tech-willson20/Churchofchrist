About Church of Chirst

Church of Chirst is a Christ-centered family dedicated to worshiping God, growing in faith, and serving our community. Founded on biblical principles and guided by the Holy Spirit, we are committed to making disciples and spreading the love of Jesus Christ.

Our church provides a welcoming environment where people from all walks of life can encounter God, develop meaningful relationships, and discover their purpose in His kingdom.

  • Spirit-filled worship services
  • Biblical teaching & discipleship
  • Children & youth programs
  • Community outreach initiatives
  • Prayer & fellowship groups

Our Ministries

Youth Ministry

Empowering teenagers (grades 6-12) to live boldly for Christ through weekly gatherings, retreats, and mentorship programs.

What We Believe

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice. It contains everything necessary for salvation and godly living.

The Triune God

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Each is fully God, co-equal and co-eternal, yet one divine being.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God who became fully human while remaining fully divine. He lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose from the dead on the third day, and ascended to heaven where He intercedes for us.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace received through faith in Jesus Christ alone. We are saved not by works, but by believing in Christ's atoning death and resurrection.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer at conversion, empowering us for holy living, gifting us for service, and producing spiritual fruit in our lives.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, composed of all believers. The local church exists to worship God, build up believers, and reach the lost with the gospel.
