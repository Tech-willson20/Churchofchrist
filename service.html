Special Services

Baptism Services

Celebrate new life in Christ through baptism. Held monthly or upon request. Preparation classes available for those interested in taking this important step of faith.

Learn About Baptism

Communion Services

Join us on the first Sunday of each month for a special time of remembrance and celebration of our Lord's sacrifice through communion.

Benevolence Services

Providing support and assistance to those in need within our community. Our benevolence ministry offers spiritual counseling, food assistance, and emergency aid to help individuals and families during difficult times.

What to Expect

Worship Style

Our worship blends contemporary and traditional elements, featuring a full worship band and choir. We sing both modern worship songs and traditional hymns.

Message

Biblical, relevant preaching that applies God's Word to daily life. Messages typically last 30-40 minutes and are available online afterward.

Children

Safe, age-appropriate programs for children from nursery through elementary during all regular services. Background-checked staff and volunteers.

Dress Code

Come as you are! Some dress casually, others more formally. We want you to feel comfortable and welcome.

Plan Your Visit

Location & Parking

Free parking available in our main lot. Handicap spaces located near each entrance. Greeters are available to help you find your way.
